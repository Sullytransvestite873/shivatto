## 🔧 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you experience problems, use these steps:

**Application does not open**
Restart your computer. This clears temporary issues that stop the software from starting.

**The assistant does not transcribe words**
Check your microphone settings in the Windows Control Panel. Ensure your microphone remains selected as the default recording device.

**Software feels slow**
Close other applications that use high amounts of memory, such as video players or internet browsers with many open tabs.

**Microphone not detected**
Unplug your headset and plug it back into the port. Verify that the software displays your current microphone in the hardware selection menu.
